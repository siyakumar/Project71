Our Achievements

Our School Garden

Made in Year 2018-2019

It's a small garden used to plant vegetables to use as a healthy snack for breakfast and lunch at North Pointe. We grow carrots, cucumbers, and other veggies. All of the classes in the school get to help with stuff like watering the garden, planting the seeds, digging up the vegetables, and other things.



Step It Up!

School Fundraiser

For those schools that don't do Step It Up, it is a fundraiser to help the school get a certain amount of money to be able to do something for the school, like a normal fundraiser, but if you donate money, you get prizes in return. You tell your parents to send at least 10 emails, and then, you get a golden ticket. You put your name, teacher's name, and class on it, and then you put how many emails you sent, and you get prizes in return. This year, we reached our goal of 25,000 dollars! Wow!

Book Fair!

Just started Book Fair! May 3-May 7. There are all kinds of books at the school Book Fair. Science Fiction, Informational Text, Fantasy, everything! There are also little erasers, pens, keychains, invisible ink, etc. Just bring some money and buy anything you want from there!

5th Grade Awards Day!
June 16

Fifth Grade Awards Day Starting June 16, 9:30 A.M. at North Pointe! It's going to be so fun since it's going to be my last year at elementary school, and I am going to be in middle school next year. All of our parents are coming to watch us and we will preform for our parents. I cannot wait!

SC Ready Testing!
May 27-28, and June 3

SC Ready Testing for 4th and 5th graders! All of us are working very hard and preparing for these end-of-the-year tests. Now that we have no more lessons for the school year, we devote all of our time to studying for these three tests, which are ELA Writing, ELA Reading, and Math.
